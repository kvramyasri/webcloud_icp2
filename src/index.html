Welcome Everyone! Let's learn how to plan trees

 “The best time to plant a tree was 20 years ago. The second best time is now.”

The following are the steps to plant a tree

  1. Dig a pit in the proper place
  2. clean near-by area and water near it
  3. Place the plant roots in the pit
  4. Close the pit and water the plant

The following are required for a plant to survive

  • a source of nutrition
  • water
  • space in which to live
  • air and optimal temperatures
